Power Edge Specs

Power Edge R710 x 2:

  • CPU: 2

  • Cores: 16

  • Disks: 4 x 2.65GB

    • 4 x RAID5

    • description: RAID bus controller, product: MegaRAID SAS 1078

    • description: SCSI Disk, size: 836GiB (898GB)

Power Edge R730:

  • CPU: 2

  • Cores: 40

  • Disks: 8 x 3.6TB

    • 1 x RAID0, 7 x RAID5

Total

  • CPU: 6

  • Cores: 72

  • Disks: 8 x 2.65GB, 8 x 3.6TB

  • Network: 12 ports
